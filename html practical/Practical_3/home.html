Zomato

Zomato, originally Foodiebay, is an Indian company founded in 2008 by Deepinder Goyal and Pankaj Chaddah, initially focusing on restaurant discovery. It has since grown into a comprehensive food-tech platform offering online ordering, table reservations, and delivery services. Zomato is known for its extensive restaurant database, user reviews, and its role in connecting consumers with local eateries. The company also engages in philanthropic activities through its "Feeding India" initiative.

Here's a more detailed look:

Early Days:
Zomato started as Foodiebay, a platform to make restaurant menus easily accessible online. This addressed the common problem of colleagues struggling to find menus at nearby restaurants.
Growth and Expansion:
Zomato expanded its services to include online ordering, table reservations, and eventually ventured into international markets.
Acquisitions:
Zomato has also made strategic acquisitions, such as Urbanspoon in 2015, which helped it enter the North American market.
Current Services:
Zomato provides restaurant discovery, online food ordering, table reservations, and a loyalty program (Zomato Gold).
Global Reach:
While headquartered in Gurugram, India, Zomato has a global presence in numerous countries.
